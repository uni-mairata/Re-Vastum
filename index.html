Raising Awareness About Recycling

Although recycling is an important process in preserving our environment, it's just as important to know how to recycle properly. Our goal is to spread this information so more of the population can understand how to contribute to solving a global problem.

Easy to Use

Since it's a web-application, our project is easily accessible on any device. The tracker, sign-in page, and our information are all mobile-friendly.

No Cost


This resource is completely free! There are no fees to use any part of this resrouce.

Quick Calculations

On our Track page, users can easily fill out the amount of recyclables they have and calculate how much of that will be recycled and how much will be turned into solid wate instantly.
